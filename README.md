## Business activities 

# Extra information: Information gathered from britannica.com and investopedia.com; "Amazon engages in a variety of business activites, including e-commerce retail sales, cloud computing through Amzon Web Serivices (AWS), and digital advertising. Additionally, it offers subscription services like Amazon Prime, sells consumer electronics such as Kindle and Echo devices, and produces original content for its Prime Video platform."

# What specific problem is the company or project trying to solve?: Information gathered from gopstack.io talks about eight problems Amazon faced and their solutions for them. The problems they faced so far this year was; comprised cash flow, high degree of competition, packaging restrictions, strict inventory expectations, high Amazon fees, counterfeiting and intellectual property issues, customer review management, and account security and fraud prevention. 

# Who is the company's intended customer? Is there any information about the market size of this set of customers?: According to coursera.org; "A company's intended customer is referred to as its target market, which is a specific group of people identified as the most likely potential customers for a product based on shared characteristics such as age, income, and lifestyle. To determine the market size of this target market, businesses often assess their total addressable market (TAM), which represents the maximum market size for their product or service."

# What solution does this company offer that their competitors do not or cannot offer? (What is the unfair advantage they utilize?)
